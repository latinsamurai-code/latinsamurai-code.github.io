Publishing

Manga Biographies

Publisher / Producer / Writer

Eiji is the founder of Emotional Content LLC., which issues worldwide "Biographic Novel" — a series of MANGA biographies through Penguin Books. Together with talented comic book artists, he describes the ways of life of heroes who had changed the world.

Subjects include the 14th Dalai Lama, Mahatma Gandhi, Mother Teresa, Che Guevara, and more.

The series is now published in over 25 countries, and in more than 10 languages.
